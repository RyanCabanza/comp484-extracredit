Hello There!

Take a sip beyond the stars...

Embark on an intergalactic adventure — destination set for the coziest place in the galaxy. Milky Way Tea Station is a space-themed boba shop and café where every drink is an immersive experience leading to uncharted worlds.

A Galaxy Full of Flavor

At Milky Way Tea Station, every order represents a different celestial destination, whether it's a vibrant planet, mysterious moon, or blazing sun. From milk teas and smoothies to acai bowls and slushes, we have a variety of choices perfect for any lifeform!

Our Mission

We welcome every customer who has a heart for exploration. Whether you’re here for a cosmic caffeine boost or just passing through on a flavor-fueled space quest, one thing’s for certain: every walk of the galaxy is welcome aboard.
